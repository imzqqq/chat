## Features

- Show recent, popular and local list of videos.
- Reproduce videos
- Login and register in your instance
- Show uploaded videos
- Subscribe to accounts
- Show your subscripcion videos
- Show your history
- Rate videos
- Show and make commentaries
- Splash screen
- Search videos
- Infinite scroll
- Share videos
- Report videos
- Peertube profiles
